What is my website about?

My website will be about Korean dish.

What is the objective of your website?

The objective of my website is to inform the visitors of my website about some of the most popular dish in Korea and provide recipe to making them. Therefore, people will be able to refer to my website to make delicious Korean food at home.

Who is your target audience set?

My target audience would be anyone who would like to experience some cultures of Korea and those who just like to cook1

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?
